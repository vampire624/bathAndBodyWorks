ADDITIONAL RETURN IN STORE GUIDELINES

All returns, exchanges and price adjustments must be made in the country of original purchase. Payment & Return Policies may vary by store locale; if the return is made with an invoice, the policy on the invoice governs. If the return is made without an invoice, the policy posted in the store of return governs.

In select stores (excluding Massachusetts), a government-issued ID is required for all returns and exchanges. Bath & Body Works will electronically scan this ID for the sole purpose of preventing return abuse. Bath & Body Works does not sell the information obtained through this process. You will be limited to $250 in non-receipted returns within a 90 day period (this does not include defective merchandise).

STEP 1: Complete the return form on the bottom of your invoice. Enclose the return form inside the box and keep the top half of the invoice for your records.

STEP 2: Prepare your package for shipment by ensuring your merchandise & paperwork are included.

STEP 3: Be sure that all return packages are properly insured and tracked. Return shipping fees are the customer’s expense. Mail your package to:

Bath & Body Works, Returns Department, 7603 Trade Port Drive, Louisville, KY 40258.

Keep the tracking information until your return has been processed. Please allow up to 21 days for us to receive your return and process your credit.

ADDITIONAL RETURN BY MAIL GUIDELINES

All returns will be credited to the original form of payment (excluding shipping and handling charges). If you paid with a Gift Card or E-Gift Card, a new E-Gift Card will be issued and emailed to you. All applicable promotions, discounts, offers, free items (as part of a qualifying purchase) and coupons granted at the time of purchase will be prorated and applied to the refund amount.
